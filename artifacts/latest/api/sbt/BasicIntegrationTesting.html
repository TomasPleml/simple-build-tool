trait BasicIntegrationTesting
extends ScalaIntegrationTesting with IntegrationTestPaths with BasicDependencyProject
A fully featured integration testing that may be mixed in with any subclass of BasicScalaProject. Pre-suite setup and post-suite cleanup are provide by overriding pretests and posttests respectively.
